About me

In the halls of technology, surrounded by blinking circuits and humming machines, I felt the urge for something new, something that would really ignite my creativity and passion. So I decided to leave the rigid confines of electronics and embrace the exciting world of front-end development.

Through an intensive bootcamp for software development, recommended by an experienced programmer, I discovered my ability to not only write code, but also to design captivating user interfaces that appeal to emotions and inspire users.

My journey has taught me that the biggest breakthroughs often come from the boldest decisions. Now I am ready to bring my skills, my passion and my vision to your company - ready to push the boundaries of what is possible and shape the future of the digital age together.
